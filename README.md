🌸 **Ferns and Petals Sales Analysis | Excel Dashboard Project**

Turning raw data into meaningful business insights — here’s my latest Excel dashboard project based on Ferns and Petals (FNP), a gifting company specializing in occasion-based deliveries like Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Valentine’s Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover insights related to:

• Revenue trends
• Customer purchasing behavior
• Product performance
• Occasion-based sales analysis
• Delivery efficiency

The goal was to design a clean, interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company wants to optimize its sales strategy and improve customer satisfaction by analyzing:

* Total Revenue
* Average Order Value
* Average Delivery Time
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Top Revenue-Generating Products
* Sales Performance of Top 5 Products
* Top 10 Cities by Orders
* Revenue by Occasion
* Order Quantity vs Delivery Time
* Product Popularity by Occasion

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

**Microsoft Excel**

* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Modeling in Excel (structured data relationships & logical organization)
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* KPI Cards
* Slicers (Month & Occasion Filters)
* Interactive Dashboard Design
* Business Data Visualization

**Visual Analysis Includes:**
• Month-wise Revenue Trend

• Top 5 Products by Revenue
• Category-wise Revenue
• Top 10 Cities by Orders
• Hour-wise Revenue Analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

• Sales peak during major occasions like Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan
• A small group of products drives a significant portion of revenue
• Metro cities contribute the highest order volume
• Evening hours generate higher revenue compared to early morning
• Delivery time remains stable around 5–6 days
